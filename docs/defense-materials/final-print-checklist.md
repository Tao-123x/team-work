## Exact Print Count Summary

Minimum practical set:

- `Main Archive Pack`: 26 pages if front matter is ready
- `Individual Handwritten Packets`: 30 pages total
- `Team Leader extra handwritten pages`: 5 pages
- `Reference Pack`: optional 25 pages from post-meeting personal notes

Recommended print totals:

- `Main Archive Pack`: 1 full copy
- `Individual Handwritten Packets`: 1 packet per member
- `Reference Pack`: 1 shared copy

## Fastest Priority Order

If printing time is tight, print in this order:

1. Week 1 main pack pages
2. Week 2 main pack pages
3. Meeting 5 main pack pages
4. All five members' Week 1 personal pages
5. All five members' Week 2 personal pages
6. All five members' Meeting 5 personal pages
7. Week 3 and Week 4 main pack pages
8. Remaining personal pages
9. Reference pack

## Live Defense Use

Use this order when showing paper evidence:

1. open the main archive to Meeting 1
2. show Meeting 2 if asked about pivot
3. show Meeting 5 if asked about preparation and teamwork
4. use individual packets only when the teacher asks who wrote what
5. use the reference pack only for detailed follow-up
